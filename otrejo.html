  Summary

 

Orlando Trejo is a professor of engineering at the Electronics and Circuits Department at Universidad Simón Bolívar, Caracas - Venezuela. Member of the Industrial Power Electronics Systems Group (GSIEP-USB), his research areas are focused in modeling and integration of renewable energy systems, electrical machines drives and power electronics converters. Additionally, he has years of field experience as field engineer in the railway and pharmaceutical industries. He also performs as co-instructor for the Spanish version of the popular MOOC at Coursera: Learning How to Learn.
